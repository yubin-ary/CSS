Breaking News: Coffee Founds Out Toxic

Coffee Extracts Show Potential Toxicity in Lab
by John Kennedy
“Coffee Found Toxic?”—Debate grows after a small study reported potential cytotoxic effects in coffee extracts. Experts caution the findings are preliminary and not directly applicable to everyday consumption, urging larger, peer-reviewed research.
Until more robust evidence emerges, nutrition authorities maintain that moderate coffee consumption remains safe for most healthy adults.
